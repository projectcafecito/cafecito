Project Cafecito / Espresso

Business Intelligence Suite

Espresso is part of a larger product suite that includes the Espresso API and Espresso Publications (editorials, X, Threads) — curated digests and deeper human-readable analysis built on the same high-signal intelligence layer. Think of it as concentrated shots of structured intelligence: event digests, synthesized signals, tags, and relationships — served fresh for dashboards, monitoring workflows, and AI agents.

Why Espresso

☕ Curated Intelligence

Event and signal sips — structured digests distilled from the noise. Get briefing-ready intelligence instead of wading through a raw news firehose.

🧠 Semantic Search

Natural-language queries with a tunable similarity threshold. Ask questions in plain text and pull deeply relevant events and signals for AI agents, RAG systems, and monitoring workflows.

🏷️ Tag Filtering

AND-filter events and signals by topics, domains, regions, and entities. Combine tags with date ranges for precise, repeatable intelligence pulls.

🔗 Relationship Graph

Traverse same_as and derived_from links between related sips. Follow how events connect to downstream signals and equivalent records across the graph.

🤖 MCP-Friendly

Return structured JSON or flat plain text with response_type=text. Lower token cost for MCP tools and LLM workflows that do not need JSON syntax.

📰 Publications Layer

Human-readable editorials and social digests on the same intelligence stack. Read curated analysis on the web, X, and Threads — or wire the API into your own tools.
